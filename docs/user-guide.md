# HPC Share - User Guide (Investors)

## Overview

This guide is for investors who want to purchase HPC tokens and earn passive yield from GPU compute leases.

## Getting Started

### Step 1: Set Up Wallet

1. Install MetaMask browser extension
2. Create or import a wallet
3. Add Polygon network (Chain ID: 137)
4. Fund wallet with MATIC for gas fees

### Step 2: Complete KYC

1. Navigate to the Investor Portal (`user.html`)
2. Click "Complete KYC on Brickken"
3. Follow Brickken's identity verification process
4. Wait for approval (typically 24-48 hours)

### Step 3: Purchase HPC Tokens

1. After KYC approval, return to the Investor Portal
2. Navigate to the "Invest" tab
3. Enter amount in EUR (minimum €100)
4. Complete payment via credit card or crypto
5. Tokens will appear in your wallet

## Managing Your Investment

### Dashboard

| Metric | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| HPC Balance | Number of tokens you own |
| Portfolio Value | Current market value of holdings |
| Total Distributions | Cumulative USDC received |
| Next Distribution | Estimated next payout |

### Distributions

- Distributions are paid quarterly in USDC
- Amount is proportional to your HPC holdings
- Automatically sent to your connected wallet
- Distribution history available in portal

### Secondary Market

- Sell tokens on Brickken P2P marketplace
- Set your own price
- Instant settlement

### Tax Reporting

- Annual tax report available in January
- Download from "Account" tab
- Includes capital gains and dividend income

## FAQ

### What is the expected yield?

Estimated 8-15% annual yield, depending on GPU utilization and market rates.

### How often are distributions paid?

Quarterly, within 30 days of quarter end.

### Can I lose money?

Yes. GPU hardware may depreciate, compute demand may fall, or operating costs may increase.

### Is this regulated?

Yes. HPC tokens are regulated security tokens issued via Brickken (CNMV-approved).

### How do I sell my tokens?

Use Brickken's P2P marketplace. A secondary market listing on 21X is planned.

### What happens if the SPV dissolves?

Token holders receive proportional share of liquidation proceeds.
