## Regulatory Status

### Spain (CNMV)

- HPC Share tokens are **regulated security tokens** under Spanish law
- Issued via Brickken, which holds CNMV sandbox approval
- Investors must complete KYC/AML
- Accredited investor requirements for certain jurisdictions

### Token Classification

| Criteria | Classification |
|----------|----------------|
| Legal nature | Equity security |
| Transferability | Restricted (whitelist only) |
| Secondary market | Permitted (P2P via Brickken) |
| Tax treatment | Capital gains + dividend income |

## Tax Implications (Spain)

| Event | Tax Rate | Notes |
|-------|----------|-------|
| Share purchase | 0% | No tax on purchase |
| Dividends | 19-23% | Withheld at source |
| Capital gains (sale) | 19-23% | On secondary market |
| Wealth tax | 0.2-3.5% | If total assets exceed €700k |

*Tax rates vary by jurisdiction. Consult local advisor.*

## Jurisdictional Notes

### EU Investors (Recommended)
- Simplified KYC
- No accreditation required for most EU countries
- Lower transfer restrictions

### Non-EU Investors
- Accredited investor verification required
- Additional KYC/AML documentation
- May have limited secondary market access

### US Investors
- Not currently accepted (SEC restrictions)
- Future Reg D offering possible
