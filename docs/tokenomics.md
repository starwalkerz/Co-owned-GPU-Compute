## Revenue Model

### Income Sources

1. **Nosana compute leases** - Primary revenue. AI inference jobs run on the GPU cluster.
2. **NOS staking rewards** - If NOS tokens are held, staking yields additional returns.
3. **Direct enterprise leases** - Secondary channel for guaranteed compute time.

### Cost Structure

| Cost Item | Percentage of Revenue |
|-----------|----------------------|
| Electricity | 10-15% |
| Hosting & maintenance | 5-10% |
| SPV management fee | 2% |
| Nosana provider fee | 5% (market rate) |
| **Net distributable** | **~70-80%** |

## Distribution Schedule

- **Frequency:** Quarterly
- **Calculation:** Based on net revenue from previous quarter
- **Payment:** USDC via Brickken automated distribution
- **Tax treatment:** Dividend income (varies by jurisdiction)

## Token Holder Rights

| Right | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Economic** | Proportional share of net revenue |
| **Information** | Quarterly reports (utilization, revenue, expenses) |
| **Liquidation** | Proportional share of hardware if SPV dissolves |
| **Governance** | Via Brickken voting (future feature) |
| **Secondary market** | P2P trading via Brickken platform |

## Secondary Market

- **Platform:** Brickken P2P marketplace
- **Liquidity:** Investor-to-investor trades
- **Future:** Potential 21X listing (institutional liquidity)
