Frequently Asked Questions

▶ What is the minimum investment?
The minimum investment is €100 (100 HPC tokens). This makes GPU compute ownership accessible to retail investors.
▶ How do I receive distributions?
Distributions are sent automatically in USDC to your connected wallet each quarter. Brickken handles the distribution smart contract.
▶ Can I sell my tokens?
Yes. HPC tokens are tradeable on Brickken's P2P secondary market. Future listing on 21X for institutional liquidity is planned.
▶ What happens to the hardware if the SPV dissolves?
Token holders receive proportional share of liquidation proceeds. Hardware is sold, USDC distributed.
▶ Is this regulated?
Yes. HPC tokens are issued via Brickken, which holds CNMV (Spanish securities regulator) sandbox approval. Full KYC/AML compliance.
